About Us

Ideal Foods and Catering (Ideal Caterers) has been a benchmark for culinary excellence in Kerala since its inception in 1984. Established by A. M. Kabir, our foundation was built upon a core commitment: to deliver cuisine that respects tradition, captivates the palate, and fosters community.

We are an integral division of the A. S. Bava Family Group, a distinguished legacy enterprise founded in 1894 at Broadway, Ernakulam, a historically significant commercial hub within Kochi.

In 2013, we strategically diversified our operations beyond catering by introducing a line of ready-to-cook gravy pastes for commercial applications. This initiative was developed through a technical collaboration with CIFT Kochi, positioning us as one of the pioneering incubatees to successfully commercialize a product under the brand name Freedom Kitchen.

Presently, we provide efficient kitchen solutions tailored for small and medium-sized restaurants and canteens. Our ready-to-use products guarantee consistent flavor profiles and exceptional hygiene standards, thereby eliminating the necessity for specialized culinary expertise. Leveraging over 40 years of industry experience, coupled with advanced blast freezing and retort technology, we meticulously preserve natural flavors and nutritional integrity, ensuring seamless and efficient kitchen operations.
